Specialties

Keratin Treatment

What it is:

Keratin is a protein that is naturally in your hair. Our in-salon treatment infuses keratin deep into your hair's cuticles, rebuilding and restoring all types of hair.

What it does:

You can forget about frizzy after this treatment! Our keratin treatment reduces up to 90% of frizz. You can even walk out in light rain, high humidity environments, and your hair will not frizz out. Be able to dry and style your hair in half the time! Our keratin treatment instantly provides amazing shine along with healthier looking hair.

How long it lasts:

With proper care, this treatment will last up to 12 weeks. After you get the keratin treatment, you will need to follow up with sulfate free shampoo to maintain and preserve the keratin treatment in your hair.

Japanese Straightening

What it is:

Japanese straightening will chemically relax your hair, leaving it silky smooth and super straight. Using thermal energy and a calcium based hydroxide solution, you can achieve that flat-ironed look in your hair with little to no effort.

What it does:

With our Japanese straightening treatment, you can achieve permanent straight hair with no effort in styling. Eliminate frizz, curliness, and increase hair luster. This innovative treatment creates humidity proof and frizz free hair no matter what type of environment you are in.

How long it lasts:

With the Japanese straightening treatment, your hair will remain permanently straight until it is grown out.

Digital Perm

What it is:

Digital perm is a technique that brings out a more natural wave to your hair. Unlike the more conventional perms, digital perms are perms that uses hot road with temperature controlled by a machine to reshape your hair texture.

What it does:

With our digital perms, we would like to introduce more of a moderately relaxed, tousled wave. It often gives you a shiner and smoother finish. We can customize the type of wave or curl you would like to achieve. Whether it's loose waves towards the ends of your hair or tighter curls all over, you can have more of a natural look with our digital perms.

How long it lasts:

Depending on your natural hair texture, our digital perms can last up to 6 months.
